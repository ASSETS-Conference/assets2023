General Camera-Ready Guidelines
Your final versions should include author names and affiliations, acknowledgments, and any other details that you may have omitted from the original submission for anonymization purposes. Please note that author data will eventually be pulled from PCS, so it is important that names, affiliations, and primary email addresses are stylized exactly the way you want to see them in the ACM digital library and on your paper. For example, if you use a personal email address (xyz123 [at] hotmail [dot] com) as your primary contact email in PCS, then this email will appear on your paper.
