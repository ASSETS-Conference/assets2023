The Graduate Hotel

The Graduate Hotel is the only hotel on Roosevelt Island and it is accessible. This hotel is brand new, just steps away from the conference venue, and very comfortable. We highly encourage you to stay there!

Hotel Ammenities

The Graduate Hotel has several conveniences which make it an ideal location to stay for your visit to New York City.

Accessible Rooms

First and foremost, the hotel offers excellent accessible rooms for attendees who prefer to stay at a hotel with better accomodations. See down below on how to reserve one of the exclusive rooms reserved for attendees. As an example, these rooms come equipped with ADA compliant doors, completely accessible restrooms, accessible emergency alarms (Audio and Visual), service animals are allowed into the rooms, and much more.

Panoramic Room Rooftop Bar

The Graduate Hotel is an 18 story building with an amazing Rooftop enviable view of the Manhattan skyline. The Rooftop Bar is open from 4pm to 10pm, and offers a variety of drinks and snacks.

Please visit the Panorama Room site for more details about their cocktails, incredible seafood, and more.

An important thing to note is that 21+ ID is required for entry, except for brunch time which is available to everyone.

Fitness Center

Your stay at the Graduate Hotel comes with access to their fitness center, which is open 24 hours a day. The fitness center is equipped with treadmills, ellipticals, free weights, and more.

We encourage everyone to take advantage of this fitness center, as it is a great way to stay healthy and active during your stay at the Conference.

Another great option is to take a walk or run around Roosevelt Island. The island is 2 miles long, and has a beautiful waterfront walkway. The walkway is also accessible, and has a great view of the Manhattan skyline.

Restaurant - Anything At All

Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner are served at the hotel's restaurant, Anything At All. The restaurant is open from 7am to 10pm, and offers a variety of food options. The restaurant also offers a bar, which is open from 11am to 10pm.

This excellent restaurant approaches their menu by offering a variety of options, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Their offerings change with seasons, so you will definitely have some unique meals when you eat here!

The block has several accessible rooms, which can be booked by calling the hotel (+1-929-447-4700).
